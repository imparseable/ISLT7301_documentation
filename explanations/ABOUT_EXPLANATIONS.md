# Explanation Files
Explanation files seek to deepen the reader's understanding of a subject. It's not often recognized in documentation, according to the [Diátaxis framework website](https://diataxis.fr/explanation/), and helps highlight connections between materials, the context, and issues about the subject at hand that are often omitted. It helps to think of it as a cueing for reflection, or that *"aha"* moment after understanding.

## Active reflection
When you make a connection, no matter how small, this a great place to share it.

## Naming conventions
Please name files related to the problems as best you can. If you're struggling, it's ok to rename the file (or realize that someone else may rename it), and that's *building* on each other's understanding and not *correcting*.
